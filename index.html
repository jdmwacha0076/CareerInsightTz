Get to know about Career Insight Tanzania

This is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) established in the year 2017 and fully registered in the year 2020. We enlight up the mind of the youth, we offer them a path to define their infinite future and opportunities in accordance to the 21st century skills.

  • Mission: To train, guide, mentor and expose the opportunities that are present to the youth  in our community.
  • Vision: Insightful and competent elite community in Tanzania for a secured future generation.
  • Value: Raising awareness on youth regarding the available career options and help them in identifying their career objectives.

Our Services

Career Guidance & Mentorship

We give advice on matters concering career choice to students from primary level, secondary level as well as university level.Career Insight Tanzania provides mentorship to people and help to bring the best that lies in you.

Resume and CV Writing
